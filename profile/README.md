## Everyday Uses

Because it works from your own photos, make comics mac fits a surprising range of everyday projects. Turn a
weekend trip into a comic strip, build a storyboard for a video, lay out a photo collage for a card, or document
a repair as a step-by-step how-to guide. Each project starts the same way: pick a template, drop in your images,

## Tips for Better Comics

A few habits make comic maker mac far more satisfying. Choose a template close to your final look before adding
photos, so panels are already sized sensibly. Crop images inside their panels rather than resizing the panels
themselves. Keep speech balloons short — they read better on a busy page. And use special-effect lettering
sparingly, for titles and sound effects, where it has the most impact.
